A Story

NOT OURS, JUST A STORY

People can be real bastards. It's true, and anyone who has lived in this world for more than 10 minutes can attest to this fact. It's as yet unclear why people are such bastards, and our scientists are working around the clock to solve this mistery*.

Out of this ocean of bastardness was born the idea for a new kind of organization, one which was not bound by the rules of society, propriety, or even physics**. A virtual safe haven where people could be themselves without fear of judgement or discrimination. These ideals are the pillars on which the mission of the Orzo Knight Foundation was built.

The Orzo Knight Foundation is not organized religion. It is not a cult or a self-help program. It is not even a foundation. Instead, think of us as a collection of beneficent-sounding words, nicely arranged into paragraphs next to some peaceful-looking imagary on a website. We do not exist in the same plane of reality as you do; we exist only in your mind.

CYBERSPACE TO MEATSPACE

We do not venture into meatspace ourselves, we do it through your actions. So go be nice to someone, be really fucking nice, and do it for no reason; they'll never see it coming because they'll expect you to be a bastard just like them. Maybe tell them about the Foundation, but do not identify yourself as a member because we don't have members. That last bit is very important.

JOINING THE CAUSE

We're doing just fine, thank you. We don't need you coming in and trying to “help” by sharing your ideas and trying to change things. We like things the way they are, we are adverse to change. So stay over there on your side of the internet, be nice to people, and do good things all on your own. But you can't do it with us.

** This is as untrue as is it impossible.
